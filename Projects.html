Probabilistic Verification of Bioreceptors in Biosensors

For different glucose and GOx quantities we have answered these questions

  • Expected Time:
    - What’s the expected time to reach a steady state (equilibrium)?
    - What’s the expected life of the bioreceptor?
  • Reproducibility:
    - What’s the accuracy and precision of the # of electrons produced at steady state -- the expected value and the standard deviation of the # of electrons at steady state?
  • Linearity:
    - Do the expected # of electrons produced at steady states change linearly?
    - What’s the max glucose quantity that can be quantized linearly i.e. the linear quantifiable limit (LQL)?
  • Sensitivity:
    - What’s the maximum glucose quantity that can be quantified -- quantifiable limit (QFL)?

Education Wing

The Education Wing of DGB Technologies provides enterprise training to upskill young generation and as well as to corporate staff designed with leading academics and experienced industry partners in a wide verity of disciplines with-in IT and Computer Science.

The Education Wing takes aspiring individuals/teams from a basic introduction of technologies such as AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Cloud Computing, Advance Software Engineering, Formal Methods, Distributed Data Processing etc. to mastery of the skills needed to build large scale solutions by deploying them in production and bringing real value to organisations.

It distinguishes itself by a focus on applied industrial solutions for teams that allows them to learn the skills, and understand the opportunities and challenges of deploying technologically advanced solutions for business outcomes.

Taught by a faculty of experienced scientists and consultants, the Education Wing provides both a strong theoretical foundation and invaluable practical knowledge based on DGB’s global experience in deploying technological solutions.
